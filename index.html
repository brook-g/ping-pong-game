Noggle Pong

Welcome to Noggle Pong! You control the left paddle with your mouse. Your goal is to hit the ⌐◨-◨ back to your opponent's side without letting it pass your paddle. The right paddle is controlled by a simple AI that will try to hit the ⌐◨-◨ back to your side. First player to score 10 points wins the game. Good luck!
